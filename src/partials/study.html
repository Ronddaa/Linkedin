Case Study

Meet Kasia, a client from Poland who initially hesitated to embrace LinkedIn. In our consultation, her skepticism echoed as she preferred popular local job websites like “Pracuj.pl and OLX”.  Curious, I asked, "Are you getting results?" Her response was a resounding "no," prompting a deeper conversation about unlocking LinkedIn's potential.

During our consultation, I took the time to understand Kasia's unique career goals and aspirations. I explained how LinkedIn could serve as a transformative tool, enabling her to connect with industry professionals, showcase her expertise, and attract the attention of potential employers.

Weeks later, Kasia returned, ready to transform her professional presence and we created her first LinkedIn profile together. A few days later our service was delivered to her, and the results were remarkable.  She secured her first job offer within 3 weeks of working with me on her profile. She was unemployed for six months.  And the journey didn't stop there, 9 additional proposals have come to her, solidifying her career trajectory.

If you find yourself resonating with Kasia's initial doubts, let me guide you towards your goals using the transformative power of LinkedIn.
